# Recognized-Territories

List of recognized territories in the noosphere &amp; what we know about them.

It is the right of any territory to opt out of monitoring, unless their influence is detected in your territory. To fully opt out of monitoring you must cease influencing any monitored areas. For more on the rules of international information warfare, refer to the [Geneva Convention / Hippocratic Oath repo](https://github.com/Prosocial-Engineering/Geneva-Convention).
